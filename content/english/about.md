
# About me

I am a PhD student doing research at the Department of Information and Communication Engineering ([DEiC](https://deic.uab.cat/)) at the Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona ([UAB](https://www.uab.cat/)). I also contribute to Bitcoin Core.
